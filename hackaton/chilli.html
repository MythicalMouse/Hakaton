Chilli con carne:

  1. Prepare your vegetables. Chop 1 large onion into small dice.
  2. Cut 1 red pepper in half lenghtways, remove stalk and wash the seeds away, then chop. Peel and finaly chop 2 garlic cloves.
  3. Start cooking. Put on medium heat. Add 1 tbsp oil and leave it for 1-2 min.
  4. Add the onion. Stir frequently until onion is soft.
  5. Tip in the garlic, pepper, 1 tbsp chilli powder.
